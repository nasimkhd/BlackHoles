Brief Defenition

Black Holes are one of the mysterious objects in the universe. By the time that the idea of black holes were born, Einstein was aware about them and had an mathematical equation about it which would show the result of their existence. Years after his death, Karl Schwarzschild officially announced the existence of black hole as the result of the general relativity.

Inside the Black Hole

Even though mankind has reached so far in the technology, just recently in last year we could spot a black hole in the space and get a picture from it which is not too transparent. This black hole is 27000 light years away from us. Due to the far distance in the space, we haven’t had the chance to identify them and research inside of it but we have lots of theory about them. The are few things that we have to know about the structure of the black holes. The boundary of the black hole is event horizon. The point that even the light with 300,000km/s cannot scape from it. When an object pass the event horizon it will be pulled inside of the black hole and goes deep down until it will be arrived at the singularity which is kind of impossible. The singularity is point that the gravity will be infinite and we have no other information than that.

What is Black Hole in the Simplified Language?

When a star dies by consuming all of its energy, all the mass of the star will be gathered in a small and tight point in the spacetime and suddenly a massive explosion happens in the space and in this situation there will be two options for the fate of the star: In the first scene, the star is transformed to a super nova which is a new generation star with different components. The second scene depends on the mass of the star, the star is transformed to a black hole. Black holes have a lot of mass which affects the spacetime and the insane gravity of the black hole can suck everything inside of it if the objects go further than the boundary of the black holes which is called Event Horizon.
